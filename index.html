✨ What is WhyNot?

WhyNot is an iOS app designed to help you break routine and live more in the present through small, meaningful daily challenges.

🎯 Features

🎉 Daily Challenges

Explore fun, generosity, culture, and discipline challenges every day.

🤝 Personalized Experience

Create your own categories and challenges tailored to your lifestyle.

🔥 Swipe Interaction

Discover new challenges with an intuitive swipe-based interface.

🎲 Random Mode

Let the app surprise you with random ideas when you want something new.

📊 Progress Tracking

Track your growth across categories like fun, discipline, and generosity.

🧠 Self Discovery

Start with a short quiz to discover your “character”.

💡 Why WhyNot?

The idea was born from a simple observation: we are documenting life more than actually living it.

WhyNot exists to help you pause, explore, and reconnect with the present — one small action at a time.
