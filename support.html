About WhyNot

WhyNot is an app designed to help you live more in the present through simple daily challenges across categories like fun, generosity, culture, and discipline.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the app?

Simply swipe through challenges, accept one you like, and tap again once you've completed it.

Can I create my own challenges?

Yes! You can create your own categories and challenges to personalize your experience.

How do I track my progress?

Your profile shows your progress across different categories like fun, generosity, and discipline.

How do I reset my data?

You can reset your progress by reinstalling the app.
